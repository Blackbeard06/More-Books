description=This mod adds the Books from some of my favorite Authors such as [url=http://jlbourne.com][u]J.L.Bourne[/u][/url] and [url=https://stephenking.com][u]Stephen King[/u][/url]. Each book is a copy of the typical book you can find in the game. Now you can read something with a title or even collect them!
